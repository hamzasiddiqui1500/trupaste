WHO ARE WE?

Originally from Penang, since 1993

Trupaste is your go-to soup paste manufacturer that helps F&B owners prepare Penang delicacies in minutes.

We specialise in producing paste for northern style noodle dishes such as Penang prawn Mee, Curry Mee, Asam laksa, Lor Mee, Jawa Mee and Soup.

From the sourcing of raw material, to the additional man power required and to possible quality control concerns. These are all challenges that restaurant owners DO NOT need to worry about because using our cooking paste is as easy as ABC.
